About Me

Hey! I'm Charlie.

I’m a tenacious developer with skills in technology such as HTML5, CSS 3, Javascript, and React. I’ve used these environments to conceptualize, experiment, and ultimately create websites, pages, and other projects like this one!

More Info

With every project, I aim to create sleek and functional designs within my evolving capabilities. I enjoy challenges that appeal to my logical and methodical problem-solving approach. I find that every puzzle has many different solutions and the fun is in finding them while adding new tools to your repertoire that, in turn, enhance your problem-solving skills.

Outside of web development, I am a musician and a multi-instrumentalist specializing in music theory, ear training, songwriting, and music production. I am adept in technology such as Pro Tools, Logic Pro X, and Finale to name a few. As a musician for 23 years, primarily a guitarist, I have written and performed original music in a wide range of genres.

Across the board, my technological approach is to utilize shortcuts and optimize workflow in all possible situations. I love learning and getting better than I was before in all aspects of life.

In my free time, I enjoy watching movies, playing video games, having fun dinner dates, and going to concerts.
